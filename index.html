Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I place my order?
We recommend placing orders at least 3-5 days in advance for standard cakes, and 1-2 weeks for custom designed cakes, especially during peak seasons.
Do you offer delivery?
Yes! We offer delivery within the city for a nominal fee. Delivery charges vary based on location and cake size.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
We offer eggless and some sugar-free options. Please discuss your specific requirements when placing your order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept cash, bank transfer, JazzCash, and EasyPaisa.
